FAQ

Below is a list of frequently asked questions. If you can't find what you’re looking for then feel free to ask us directly via

1. What is your privacy and security policy?

A security policy is a written document in an organization outlining how to protect the organization from threats, including computer security threats, and how to handle situations when they do occur.

2. How long is the development timeline?

It should not take longer than two weeks to develop any given feature. If a feature will take longer than two weeks to build, it should be broken up into smaller features.

3. What type of companies do you work with?

Which company are you working for - Applies when you are an employee of a company (going to office or working from home) like TCS, HP, Hyundai etc.

4. How many team members for a project?

The recommended size of the team is around seven members, give or take a couple depending on the nature of the project. According to the Scrum guidelines, teams of larger sizes require too much effort for communication.

5. What warranties are to ensure deliverables are met?

Have a detailed and clear description for all team members involved in the project; Specify the operations and functionalities that will be performed by the software; Ensure alignment with all requirements provided by the customer.

6. Do you outsource work?

Outsourcing is the business practice of hiring a party outside a company to perform services or create goods that were traditionally performed in-house by the company's own employees and staff.
